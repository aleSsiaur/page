## 🔬 Research & Design Interests

> **Product-Service System Design (PSSD) & Systematic Innovation**  
> I focus on designing holistic, user-centric product-service systems. I love using design thinking tools—such as empathy mapping, user personas, and ecosystem diagrams—to solve complex social and urban challenges through systematic innovation.

> **UI/UX & Brand Visual Identity with Sleep Wellness Focus**  
> I am passionate about crafting intuitive digital interfaces and cohesive brand experiences. My recent explorations integrate sensory elements (scent, touch, and sight) into digital products, focusing on sleep wellness, holistic bedtime rituals, and thoughtful typography.
